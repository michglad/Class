How to make fish stew

This is a photograph of a properly cooked fish stew

Ingridients

  • A readily deep fried fish
  • Cooking oil
  • Onoins
  • Tomatoes
  • Black pepper
  • Dhania
  • Salt

How to prepare fish stew

  1. Place a cooking pot on the stove and add little oil
  2. Add chopped onions to the oil and allow it to turn brown
  3. Add chopped tomatoes to the misture and use a cooking stick to stir till they are soft
  4. Place the pieces of fish and add little water
  5. Add black pepper and allow it to simmer for five minutes
  6. Add chopped dhania into the pot and leave it to simmer for three minutes

Serve when hot with ugali or rice
